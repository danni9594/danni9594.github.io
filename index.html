About

Hi! My name is Danni Peng (彭丹昵).
I'm currently a Senior Researcher at Huawei Singapore Research Centre. Previously, I worked as a research scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), A*STAR. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), advised by Prof. Sinno Jialin Pan and sponsored by the Alibaba-NTU Talent Programme.
