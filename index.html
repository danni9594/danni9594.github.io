About

Hi! My name is Danni Peng (彭丹昵).
I am currently a research scientist at the Institute of High Performance Computing (IHPC), A*STAR, Singapore. Before joining IHPC, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), under the supervision of Prof. Sinno Jialin Pan and sponsored by the Alibaba-NTU Talent Programme. Previously, I received my B.Eng. (Hons.) degree from National University of Singapore (NUS).
My research focuses on multi-task/multi-distribution learning scenarios, including meta-learning, continual learning, federated learning, and domain generalization.
